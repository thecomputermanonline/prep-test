Audio script for Questions 1 - 10

Telephone conversation between a company employee and a vendor.

William: Hello, Good morning Jessica, this is William from TFN limited we spoke last week on the party for my colleague who is leaving us.

Jessica: Hi William and good morning to you too. How may I help you today?

William: well I have you on speaker here and I would just like to go over everything we discussed again so that my team can take down the details if that is fine with you.

Jessica: that’s perfectly okay William, and hello to everyone in the room.

William: Alright. So like I had earlier said, this is a party for about 200 guests to say farewell to our Chief Marketing Officer who has been with us for 42 years. He is retiring at the grand age of 65 and this party is to thank him for his years of faithful service.

Jessica: Yes. If I remember correctly, he was one of the first hires of the company and grew through the ranks, and he’s the only one in the history of the company to have done that.

William: precisely! Good memory you’ve got there. So, it starts out as a dinner party with slow music and tributes and speeches, then becomes a dance party that lasts till the early hours of the next day.

Jessica: oh? It seems the format has been changed.

William: good catch Jessica. Yes it has slightly. We had initially thought to make it a very formal occasion with the dinner party part but the younger ones on our team seem to think including an after party with dancing in it will make it a more inclusive event and a more memorable one as well.

Jessica: that’s a pretty good argument and it gives everyone a little something to look forward to as well. And if we include music from across the generations, we can ensure it is a party that everyone will enjoy.

William: our thoughts exactly! We are looking to include music from the 70’s through to music of today.

Jessica: that’s perfect! A musical journey that reflects his career years

William: I think you’ve just given us the theme for the after party.

Jessica: well that’s what I’m here for, glad to be of service.

------------------------------- Beginning of part two--------------------------------------------

William: please give us an overview of the cost items we need to be prepared for once again.

Jessica: well with an event of this size, and with the aspects mentioned, you will firstly need to have a venue that can accommodate both the dinner party and the after party in adjoining rooms. I say adjoining rooms because you want to keep the travel between venues to a minimum.

William: okay

Jessica: then of course you will need to be ready to handle costs for feeding, decoration, lighting, sound, music, …

William: ah Jessica I think we may need to set up a live meeting for all this.

Jessica: (chuckles) yes that will be best. The list is quite extensive, but as an event planner, I can help with recruiting vendors for these areas, so all you have to worry about is making the payments.

William: yes that will be perfect. Justin and Anna will be in touch with you to set up the next meeting and will be your contacts for the entire event planning.

Jessica: that’s fine with me. They can send me an email and I will confirm once I get it. I look forward to working with you all and executing a flawless event.

William: Thanks for your time Jessica and have a splendid week.

Jessica: you too William. Bye.
